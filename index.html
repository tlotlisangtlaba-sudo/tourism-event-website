Event Overview

The Maluti Mountain Adventure and Culture Festival is a tourism event that is held in the beautiful and stunning Maluti mountain region of Lesotho, in Thaba-Tseka district at Katse Dam. The purpose of this Festival is to celebrate the culture of Basotho, outdoor adventure, traditional food and live music. Additionally, the visitors from around the world can explore and experience the beautiful mountains while enjoying the cultural perfomances and the exciting adventure activities which include pony trekking, hiking, mountain biking and cultural village tours. All in all, the event aims at promoting tourism, preserving local culture, and creating opportunities for local businesses and artists.
