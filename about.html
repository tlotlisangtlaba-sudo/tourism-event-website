History and Significance

The Maluti Mountain Adventure and Culture Festival was created to promote tourism in Lesotho and showcase the beauty of the Maluti Mountains. The event highlights Basotho traditions, local food and music while encouraging adventure tourism. The Festival also supports local communities by business opportunities for local vendors and perfomers.

Target Tourists

  • Adventure Tourists
  • International Travellers
  • Local Families
  • Culture enthusiasts
  • Photographers and Nature Lovers
